WHAT IS IT?

RZ Metaversity is a decentralized campus or known as Decentralized University which has the concept of Teach to L-Earn, because we believe that knowledge and knowledge can come from anyone from anywhere and anytime.

As the name suggests, Metaversity = Metaverse + University, the focus of our activities on campus is teaching and learning activities with specific majors in the world of Cryptography and Blockchain, both in terms of macroeconomics, microeconomics and also from a business perspective

WHAT IS T2E?

T2E is short for Teach To Earn, but there is a slight modification to Earn with the addition of the letter L which stands for Learn. So T2E is Teach To L-Earn, meaning that by teaching we can earn income and at the same time increase our own knowledge.
